# About

Howdy! My name is Tom Kay (b. 1995). I am a game developer, writer, and composer based in New Jersey.

In 2022, I bought my first Game Boy. Since then I have been taking photos with my Game Boy Camera and making games for the system in GB Studio. I now own five Game Boy consoles as of this writing...

For work or other inquiries, feel free to send me an email. (You must have Javascript enabled for the email link to work correctly.) I have no other official social media or contact links other than the links listed on my home page.

Programs I use often:
- Aseprite for drawing art and game assets
- BGB Emulator for playing and testing Game Boy ROMs.
- GB Studio for making games
- hUGETracker for composing music
- Obsidian for note-taking and planning

Programs I am also familiar with:
- bitsy
- Godot
- HTML/JavaScript
- Twine (SugarCube FTW!!!)
